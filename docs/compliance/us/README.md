# United States

## Regulatory Bodies
- Securities and Exchange Commission (SEC) – oversees securities markets. [SEC](https://www.sec.gov)
- Commodity Futures Trading Commission (CFTC) – regulates derivatives and commodities. [CFTC](https://www.cftc.gov)
- Financial Crimes Enforcement Network (FinCEN) – administers Bank Secrecy Act obligations. [FinCEN](https://www.fincen.gov)
- State regulators (e.g., NYDFS) – issue money‑transmitter licences. [NYDFS BitLicense](https://www.dfs.ny.gov/apps_and_licensing/virtual_currency_businesses)

## Licensing Requirements
- Register as a Money Services Business (MSB) with FinCEN.
- Obtain state money‑transmitter licences where activities trigger state law.
- Securities tokens may require SEC registration as a broker‑dealer, alternative trading system, or other securities licence.
- Derivatives or leveraged trading may require CFTC registration.

## Token Classification
- SEC applies the **Howey Test** to determine whether a token is a security. [DAO Report](https://www.sec.gov/litigation/investreport/34-81207.pdf)
- CFTC treats many virtual currencies as commodities. [CFTC Primer](https://www.cftc.gov/sites/default/files/idc/groups/public/@customerprotection/documents/file/oceo_a_primer_on_virtual_currencies.pdf)
- FinCEN classifies convertible virtual currency as "money" for AML purposes. [2019 Guidance](https://www.fincen.gov/resources/statutes-regulations/guidance/application-fincens-regulations-certain-business-models)

## Reporting Obligations
- MSBs must file Suspicious Activity Reports (SARs) and Currency Transaction Reports (CTRs) under the Bank Secrecy Act.
- Travel Rule compliance for transfers above $3,000 per [31 CFR 1010.410(f)](https://www.ecfr.gov/current/title-31/section-1010.410).
- Certain transactions may trigger IRS information reporting (e.g., Forms 1099 or 8300).

## AML/KYC Requirements
- Implement a written AML programme including Customer Identification Program (CIP) and ongoing monitoring.
- Screen customers against OFAC sanctions lists and maintain records per BSA requirements.
